Understanding Energy Consumption in Australia

Average Household Consumption

The average Australian household consumes approximately 6,000-7,000 kWh of electricity per year, with appliances accounting for about 33% of total energy use.

Energy Star Ratings

Look for appliances with high Energy Star ratings (4-6 stars) to maximize efficiency. A 6-star appliance can use up to 50% less energy than a 1-star model.

Peak Usage Times

In Australia, peak electricity usage typically occurs between 6-8 PM. Energy-efficient appliances help reduce strain during these costly peak periods.

Top Energy-Consuming Appliances

Air Conditioning ~40% of home energy use
Water Heating ~25% of home energy use
Refrigeration ~8% of home energy use
Televisions & Electronics ~5-7% of home energy use
